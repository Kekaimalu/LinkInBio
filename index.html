Fact Checking Websites

News Guard

A site and browser extension dedicated to News Literacy and battling misinformation on the Internet. News Guard has a real time updated list of common misinformation spread about the COVID-19 Pandemic.

Politifact

Independent site dedicated to non-partisan fact-checking of journalism. Politifact has been fact-checking rumors, conspiracies, and misinformation on the Coronavirus Pandemic and has a dedicated page for all articles on that topic.

Snopes

An independent publication dedicated to investigatin & fact-checking claims. Snopes has a dedicated collection of articles dedicated to fact-checking misinformation on COVID-19 claims such as Mask & Vaccine hesitancy.

All Sides

A News aggregate that wants to expose people to how information and ideas about the world are presented from all sides of Socio-Political discource. All Sides has a page dedicated to the Coronavirus and how it is portrayed across the political spectrum.
